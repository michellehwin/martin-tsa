About Brontes

Our Philosophy

Here at Brontes Automotive, we’ve made it our mission to facilitate a global movement toward sustainable energy. The Earth is deteriorating, and it’s our duty to preserve it. With emissions and air pollution on the rise, it’s evident that gasoline cars are not contributing to the solution.

With this in mind, electric cars are the ideal answer to our vehicle emissions. Brontes’ vehicles are made to transition the world to a zero-emission future. Not only are these cars eco friendly, but they are powerful. The tradeoffs from gasoline to electric cars have become nonexistent. Study after study, our cars prove to be, in actuality, superior to gasoline cars. Our cars excel in all categories from speed to safety. This is the start of the transportation we need. Brontes will only continue to grow and enhance its technology as we find the best route to a better future.

DESIGN

Collision Detection

All of Brontes’ vehicles have exceptional collision detection with 14 ultrasonic sensors, a radar, and 360 degree camera sight. These vehicles will sense cars before the driver does and guarantee a safe drive.

Autopilot

Brontes vehicles all support autopilot, and are able to navigate the streets with minimal to no human assistance. The user-friendly interface and advanced sensory systems allows everyone to take advantage of this feature.

Green Engines

Brontes’ award winning electrical engines are installed in every car. Say goodbye to emission and weak gasoline engines. These engines run on the vehicle’s battery pack and are designed to maximize energy.

Tough Shells

Brontes produces its car shells out of a steel-titanium alloy, the strongest and most efficient combination in the market. Brontes cars are resistant to the wear and tear of the world. This shell protects against all dangers, ranging from scratches to impacts.

Auto-Dimming Mirrors

The rear view mirrors on the vehicles are all light-sensitive. This allows them to adapt to the environment’s lighting and darken to suppress glare. Bid farewell blinding glares and driver distraction.

Our Company

Founded in 2001, Brontes has always aspired to discover new and innovative ways of using sustainable energy. The founders not only wanted to prove that electric cars could compete with gasoline but even replace them. Years of research and planning would happen before our first car release in 2003. The vehicles were an immediate success and revealed cutting-edge electric technology. The original model swept the market and ranked extremely high among all categories.

Since then, we have only expanded, and now sell four world-class vehicles. These cars have proven to be the best among all, breaking records from fastest acceleration to the furthest range. Electric cars are not only becoming better but more affordable and accessible. Brontes continues to shatter the world’s expectations for electric vehicles and push the horizons for technology.

Sasha Trinh, Founder

Sasha Quynh Trinh was gifted in engineering and immediately began running designs for electrical cars after completing her phD in aerospace and automotive engineering. She had a vision for a cleaner world and embarked upon all opportunities to see it through. Trinh gathered others who shared her aspirations and founded Brontes in 2001 and has since revolutionized transportation as we know it.

Matthew Henderson, CEO

Matthew James Henderson worked closely alongside Sasha during the early days of Brontes. He held the title of chief engineer for years until Sasha’s retirement. Brontes would not see its success without Henderon’s vision. The company has risen to the number one manufacturer for electrical cars under his lead and racked up numerous awards.

Jessica Le, Chief Engineer

Jessica Le has an extensive background in both energy and engineering. She sits on the head of the American Council for Renewable Energy and has worked in automotive engineering for years. Her passion and intellect has time and time produced groundbreaking designs. Brontes has earned a myriad of titles through her innovations.
