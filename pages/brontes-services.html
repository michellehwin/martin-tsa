Warranty Policy

Upon purchase, your vehicle is protected by a 5 year/ 75,000 mile warranty. The car battery, engines, and driving unit are covered for:

  • Model 1 - 150,000 miles w/ minimum 60% battery retention
  • Model 2 - 200,000 miles w/ minimum 60% battery retention
  • Model 3 - 120,000 miles w/ minimum 60% battery retention
  • Model 4 - 120,000 miles w/ minimum 70% battery retention

Warranties cover any repairs, restorations, or replacements deemed necessary to correct any flaws or deficiencies resulting from normal use. Warranties are limited to parts manufactured by Brontes alone. Car defects present from the time of purchase do not require a warranty and may be immediately replaced. Please review the full warranty for further details and specifications.
